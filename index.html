Rock, Paper, Scissors

How to play

Click on rock, paper or scissors icons below and the computer will play at random. The winner's name - computer or player 1 will be revealed below. Remember! Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock and scissors beats paper. Have fun!

    • The Rock is a tough cookie to beat. He blunts Scissors so quickly that there's no chance for a getaway from Mr Scissors. He's a goner. Fear not! Rock is beaten by Paper. Paper will cover Rock making it impossible for him to see. Without sight, Rock can't last very long.
